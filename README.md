🍽️ NutriSense AI
Predictive Food Decision Coach

NutriSense AI is a real-time health intervention system designed to stop poor dietary choices before they happen. Unlike traditional calorie trackers that are reactive, NutriSense uses AI to predict "high-risk" eating moments based on your stress, location, and historical patterns.
🚀 The Problem

Most people fail their diets not because they lack knowledge, but because of momentary lapses in willpower triggered by:

    Late-night cravings (Temporal patterns).

    High-stress situations (Emotional triggers).

    Proximity to junk food (Location-based triggers).

💡 Our Solution: Predictive Intervention

NutriSense AI intercepts users 5–30 minutes before a bad decision.

    Pattern Detection: Learns that you usually order Biryani when stressed at 11 PM.

    Smart Interventions: Sends a notification offering a healthier high-protein alternative nearby or a 5-minute home recipe.

    Computer Vision: Scan any food item to get an instant health score and better alternatives.

        Google Cloud Vision: Real-time food recognition.

🗺️ Key Features

    Adaptive UI: Seamless experience across Mobile and Web.

    Habit Learning Engine: Analyzes logs to find your "Willpower Weakpoints."

    Gamification: Earn badges like "Prediction Master" and maintain "Healthy Streaks."

    Context-Aware: Suggestions based on your current budget, location, and fitness goals.
